About Our Old Student Association

Federal Government College Kwali Old Students Association(FEDGOKOSA) is a non profit and non governmental organisation registered with the Corporate Affairs COmmission(CAC) on the 9th October 2009 with number CAC/IT/NO35123. It has a constitution governing its affairs and a leadership headed by the president of the National Executive Council.
